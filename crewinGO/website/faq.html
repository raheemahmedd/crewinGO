Frequently Asked Questions

What does CREWINGO do?
 CREWINGO  is a cloud based human resources management platform designed to collect and organise all your employee related information.
Who does CREWINGO serve?
 CREWINGO  is built with small and medium sized company in mind.
How long does it take to setup CREWINGO?
Less than a minute. Once you complete the two step registration process you are in and ready to go. Everything is in place and you can start onboarding your employees.
What can I do in CREWINGO during the free trial period?
You get access to the full platform during the trial period, no restrictions!
How am I charged?
You are charged monthly per number of active employees. Simple!
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel your CREWINGO account at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of the monthly billing cycle and you will not be charged further. You will never be charged if you have cancelled during the free trial period.
